Building comparison briefs

Use a structured brief to summarize a comparison for scouting notes or team discussions.

Try a comparison Back to Learn Browse players

What you will learn

  • Summarize rate, efficiency, and role in three short bullets.
  • Call out one key advantage and one caution for each player.
  • Link back to the comparison for transparency.

Brief structure

Start with a one-line overview of the players being compared.

Add three bullets: rate highlight, efficiency highlight, and role context.

Sharing responsibly

Always label comparisons as educational and non-predictive.

Include the compare link so others can verify the numbers.

FAQ

Where should I share a brief?

Use internal channels or discussions with scouts, analysts, or fans. Avoid presenting it as a prediction or betting advice.
