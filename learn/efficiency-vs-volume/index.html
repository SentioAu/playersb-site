Efficiency vs volume

Great output can come from high volume, elite efficiency, or both. Learn to separate them.

Try a comparison Back to Learn Browse players

What you will learn

  • Volume signals opportunity; efficiency signals conversion quality.
  • Avoid overvaluing small-sample efficiency without consistent volume.
  • Compare players within similar roles to avoid misleading extremes.

Spotting volume

Shots/90 and touches in the box highlight players who generate chances frequently.

High volume without finishing may still be valuable if it keeps pressure on opponents.

Reading efficiency

Goals per shot and shot accuracy show how well a player converts chances.

Efficiency spikes can be volatile, so check multiple seasons or similar-minute samples.

FAQ

What if a player is low volume but high efficiency?

That can indicate elite finishing or a small sample. Look at minutes and role before projecting future output.
