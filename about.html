What PlayersB is

PlayersB is a player-first utility platform focused on comparison tools, performance normalization, and fantasy-safe decision support. The site is built around player entities and repeat-use tools rather than news or predictions.

  • Player A vs Player B comparison tools
  • Performance calculators (per-90, per-minute, efficiency)
  • Fantasy-oriented tools that explain scoring and tradeoffs
  • Clear, documented methodology for every metric

What PlayersB is not

  • No sportsbook or betting platform
  • No betting tips, locks, or guaranteed picks
  • No news aggregation or live-score service
  • No opaque or unexplained “AI predictions”

Our approach

We prioritize explainability and structure. When we use derived metrics (such as consistency or efficiency), inputs and assumptions are documented. Tools are designed to help users understand tradeoffs, not to tell them what to do.

Responsible use

Sports outcomes are uncertain. PlayersB tools are educational and informational. If you participate in wagering where legal, do so responsibly and within local laws.
