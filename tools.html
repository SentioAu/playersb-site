PlayersB tools are built around player entities and repeat usage. Outputs are explainable and grounded in inputs.

Player performance calculators

  • Per-90 / Per-minute calculator — normalize output by time played.
  • Points-per-game / goals-per-game — quick rate view for comparison.
  • Minutes-to-output ratio — how often a player produces per minutes played.
  • Consistency score (simple) — stability indicator based on variation around recent averages.

Player comparison

  • Player A vs Player B — compare normalized stats side-by-side with clear tradeoffs (rate, volume, stability).

Fantasy tools

  • Fantasy points calculator — scoring presets with transparent breakdown.
  • Comparison under fantasy scoring — see how scoring changes the A vs B result.
  • Squad value helper — structured view of tradeoffs (price, role, output rate).

Math tools

  • Odds format converter — decimal / American / fractional.
  • Implied probability — convert odds to implied chance.
  • Overround / vig — estimate margin from a market.
